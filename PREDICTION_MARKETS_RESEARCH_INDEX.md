## Key Findings Quick Access

### Top Performing Models
- **Sports:** XGBoost + LightGBM Ensemble → 52.6-67.7% accuracy
- **Time Series:** LSTM + Transformer Hybrid → 70-80% validation accuracy
- **Multi-sector:** Stacking Ensemble → 53-56% consistent accuracy
- **Sentiment:** LLM-Based (GPT-4) → 74.4% on returns prediction

### Critical Success Factors (Ranked)
1. Probability calibration (temperature scaling)
2. Kelly Criterion bankroll management (fractional)
3. Domain-specific feature engineering
4. Ensemble methods (minimum 4-5 base models)
5. Risk management (position limits, drawdown control)

### Performance Targets
- Brier Score: < 0.20
- Win Rate: 52-56%
- Sharpe Ratio: > 1.0
- Max Drawdown: < 25%
- Annual ROI: 10-30% realistic

### Top 5 GitHub Repos
1. kalshi-ai-trading-bot (Multi-agent system)
2. ProphitBet-Soccer-Bets-Predictor (ML pipeline)
3. awesome-prediction-markets (Resource hub)
4. NBA-Prediction-Modeling (Sports reference)
5. ArbiDex (Arbitrage detection)

### Technology Stack Essentials
- **ML:** XGBoost, LightGBM, scikit-learn, TensorFlow
- **Data:** Pandas, NumPy, Polars
- **Sentiment:** RoBERTa, FinBERT
- **APIs:** Kalshi, Polymarket, Manifold, Yahoo Finance, NewsAPI

### Most Important Insights
1. Only 3% of bettors are profitable (risk management critical)
2. Market efficiency limits achievable accuracy (~70% max)
3. Calibration > Accuracy (probability matching is key)
4. Ensemble methods consistently beat single models
5. Fractional Kelly prevents ruin better than optimization
